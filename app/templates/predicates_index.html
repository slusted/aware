What is a prediction?

A prediction is a named claim about the market with a small set of mutually-exclusive states — e.g. "do buyers prefer self-serve or sales-led?". Each prediction has a statement, a category, and a list of state labels. The forecast math (probabilities, velocity, scenario rollups) lives on the Scenarios page.

The agent reviews predictions against incoming findings and proposes refinements: rename a state, refine the wording, split a state, retire the prediction, etc. Open a prediction to see its definition and review pending proposals.
