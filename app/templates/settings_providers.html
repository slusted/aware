Content extraction

Where page bodies come from

When scanning finds a URL, we fetch the page body for the LLM to read. The fetcher has three tiers: ZenRows (paid, primary by default), ScrapingBee (paid fallback that clears Cloudflare / JS walls), and trafilatura over urllib (self-hosted, free).

Adding a key

  1. Get a Serper key at serper.dev (free tier: 2500 queries).
  2. Add to .env: SERPER_API_KEY=...
  3. Optional: override pricing with SERPER_USD_PER_CALL=0.0003 (default).
  4. Restart the server, then come back here and click Enable.
